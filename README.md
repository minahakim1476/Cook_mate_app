CookMate is an AI-powered mobile application that helps users discover recipes and daily meal ideas.  
You can chat with the AI to get recipe suggestions based on the ingredients you already have, or simply ask for new food ideas.  

---

## Features  

**AI Chat**  
Interact with the AI assistant to get recipe recommendations or meal ideas tailored to your available ingredients.  

**Daily Ideas**  
A dedicated page that provides fresh meal inspirations every day to make cooking easier and more exciting.  

**Food List**  
Create your own food list by saving recipes with their names and ingredients for quick access anytime.  

---

## User Flow  

1. Chat with AI ‚Üí Get meal or recipe suggestions.  
2. Explore Daily Ideas ‚Üí Discover automatically updated food ideas.  
3. Manage Food List ‚Üí Save your favorite meals and their ingredients.
